Monthless Calendar Proposal

Today is ...

... but should be .

Comparison - Full Calendar

Why ?

  • Mental overhead to keep track of the date (due to months irregularities)
  • Moon phases are not really relevant anymore
  • Often replaced by week multiples
  • I can't ever remember which month it is

Proposal

  • 52 weeks of 7 days
  • Plus one Lastday
  • Plus one optional Leapday
  • All years start on a Monday (weeks will be the same every year)
